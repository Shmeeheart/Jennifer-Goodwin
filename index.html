...Jennifer was born in a large family who reveled in nick-naming each other. No one knows how "Jennifer" warped into "Shmee" as a nickname, but it stuck throughout the years. Shmee can be spicy at times, but mainly she is a Shmeeheart ❤️

Jennifer has a natural curiosity for life and a desire to do and know everthing, which allows her to relish in many hobbies and explore various career paths. Technology is an area that has always sparked Jennifer's interest, but had not taken the steps to pursue education and experience further...until now. No matter what career, Jennifer believes that staying abreast of technological advances is imperative to success in the modern world. Her inquisitive nature has opened up the door to a new career in technology. The unknown is both exciting and terrifying and Jennifer welcomes the challenge.

Outside of work, you can find Jennifer rocking out at a concert, in the audience of a Broadway Musical, fishing, reading, listening to true crime podcasts, playing her guitar, bowling, gardening or singing!
